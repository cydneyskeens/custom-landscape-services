Our Fleet

Equipment to tackle most landscaping, maintenance, and dirt work. Highlights of our fleet include:

  • Big John tree spade truck capable of transplanting trees up to 8' diameter
  • Tight access Vermeer tree spade
  • Implements to fulfill most landscape needs from mowing to plowing and planting
  • Specialized hose track planer, road grader, and snow plow

The Farm

We operate a tree farm in Northeast Arkansas where we plant and nurture native seedlings. Trees include bald cypress, various species of pine, hardwood trees, nut trees including pecan and black walnut, and many species of oak and ornamentals like dogwood and cedar. We also have a small fruit orchard with many varieties of apple, plum, peach, and fig.
