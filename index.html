About Custom Landscape Services

Custom Landscape Services

Services

Landscaping firm specializing in reforestation, tree transplanting, native grassland preservation. Our services range from landscaping and horticultural consultations, concept sketches, material selection / sourcing, planting plans and site layout all the way to install.

Our Fleet

Equipment to tackle most landscaping, maintenance and dirt work. Highlights of our fleet including: Big John tree spade truck capable of transplanting trees up to 8" diameter tight access Vermeer tree spade implements to fulfill most landscape needs from mowing to plowing and planting, specialized horse track planer, road grader and snow plow

The Farm

We operate a tree farm in North East Arkansas where we plant and nurture native seedlings. Trees include baldcypress, various species of pine, hardwood trees, nut trees including pecan and black walnut, many species of oak, and ornamental trees like dogwood and redbud. We also have a small fruit orchard with many varietals of apple, plum, peach, and fig.
